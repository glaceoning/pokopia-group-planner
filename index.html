        How to use it

        A clearer workflow

        1. Start by adding the Pokémon that are already in your Pokopia group.
        2. On mobile, the fastest option is usually pasting a list with one Pokémon name per line.
        3. Use the filters only if you want recommendations that fit a specific habitat or specialty.
        4. Read the recommendation scores from left to right: bigger combined scores are better fits for your current team setup.
        5. Tap Add on any recommendation to move that Pokémon straight into your current group.

        How scoring works

        What the numbers mean

        • Habitat score adds points for matching habitats and subtracts points for habitats that directly conflict.
        • Favorites score adds points based on how many favorite Pokémon a candidate shares with your current team.
        • The slider lets you decide whether habitat or favorites should influence the ranking more.
        • The overlap section helps you quickly see where your existing group is already clustering too heavily.
