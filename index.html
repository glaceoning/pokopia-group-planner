        How this works now

        Owned-first planning, finally

        • Imported Pokémon are automatically added to your in-app Pokédex.
        • Your owned Pokédex is the default source for squad building.
        • Recommendations default to owned Pokémon and can optionally expand to unowned ones.
        • Unowned recommendations are clearly marked so there is no confusion.

        Scoring model

        Still transparent, now easier to read

        • Habitat overlap boosts matching environments and penalizes direct habitat conflicts.
        • Favorites overlap rewards candidates that align with what your squad already likes.
        • The weighting slider lets you decide whether habitat or favorites matters more.
        • Synergy cards and overlap panels show why the squad is or is not coherent.
